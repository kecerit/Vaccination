Some Terminology


The next part of the questionnaire requires you to understand some terms. Even though you may already be familiar with them, we explain a few important terms here and ask you to read them carefully.

Infection Rate: The infection rate tells you how many people an infected person infects on average. E.g., an infection rate of 2 would mean that every infected person infects 2 other people on average.

Death Rate: The death rate tells you the percentage of people who got the disease and then died because of it. E.g., a death rate of 5% would mean that 5% of those having the disease die.

Vaccinated Population Rate: This number tells you the percentage of the population which has already received a vaccination against the specific disease. E.g., a vaccination rate of 90% would mean that 90% of the population are vaccinated.
